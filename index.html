About Me

“In supply chain, the only constant is change; therefore, the ability to forecast and plan effectively is paramount to success”

This quote by Robert A. Burgelman resonates with me very well when I think about Operations and Supply Chain Management because having a proper supply and demand plan ensures positive customer satisfaction and avoids any additional costs within the whole network of supply chain partners.

Hello! I'm Pallavi Khabale, a Supply Chain enthusisast currently pursuing a Master's degree in Supply Chain Management from University of Washington's Michael G. Foster School of Business. I'm looking forward to leverage my skills as a former SAP Integration consultant along with the functional supply chain knowledge.

With 3+ years of experience as SAP Cloud Platform Integration Consultant (SAP CPI-DS) for SAP IBP, I have successfully led 4 global end-to-end business separation and data integration projects. Notably, I delivered a solution to generate stock transfer receipts for 30-40% of articles sourced from external vendors that improved the forecast accuracy for the suppliers. Additionally, I spearheaded a currency changeover project, ensuring a smooth transition of key transactional data from local currency to euros with no business disruption. My role involved ensuring smooth dataflow across systems while collaborating with stakeholders, analyzing data and optimizing ETL logic to meet business needs.

My tenure at Accenture gave me opportunities to experience first hand the criticalness of connecting businesses with customers and the overall complex nature of the process. Whether it's solving complex integration challenges or optimizing processes for efficiency, I thrive on delivering data driven solutions that align with the organization's goals.

My strengths lie in designing data flows for demand and supply forecasting, process improvement and inventory optimization. Skilled in SAP CPI-DS, SAP IBP, SAP Basis, Advanced Excel, SQL, BI, Tableau and other MS-Office Tools, I have a solid technical foundation that enables me to deliver strategic solutions. Key strengths that define me include being an achiever, highly focused and being detail oriented. Moreover, I enjoy fostering a collaborative environment and have a never-ending spirit for learning.

Outside of professional pursuits, I am a K-pop, K-drama, and anime enthusiast. I also love hiking and am eager to explore the breathtaking landscapes of the Pacific Northwest to the fullest.
