Living the Dream

Hello everyone! My name is Pallavi. Born on November 2nd in Mumbai. Back in June 2023, moving to the USA wasn't even in the books but fast forward to June 2024, I moved to Seattle to begin my master's degree at the University of Washington.

What drove me to take this decision you may ask? Well I was fascinated with the world of supply chain that I got to experience at Accenture as an SAP Integration Consultant. Oh yeah and I love learning. I spent 3+ years to learn German for my client company. Not gonna lie it was difficult. But I'm proud to reach B1 Level. A little secret - I always wanted to move abroad. Seeing the world, was and is always my dream.

Documenting my journey along the way is my favourite pass time. Capturing the happy moments and weaving videos together is a passion of mine. I still think I am a newbie at that but practice maketh (wo)man.

I am a huge huge K-pop, K-drama and anime fan. Seventeen and BTS are my favourite k-pop groups. Their passion towards music and art always inspires me to do my utmost best. Their songs give me that sense of healing and comfort on my difficult days. I finally got to attend my first ever K-pop concert. None other than SEVENTEEN. Believe me when I say those two days were the best days of my life. I was absolutely in awe and in tears by the end of the concert. What makes it more memorable was that the first day of concert was an impromptu decision made just an hour before the concert. I got tier one tickets much closer to the stage, and it still feels like a dream.

This year was marked with a lot of firsts. First international flight, moving abroad, living alone, and being independent - you name it. One of the highlights for the past 7 months is I took a solo trip to San Franscisco after my concert in Oakland. Truly a learning experience.

Here's the Golden Gate Bridge. Not hidden by Carl the fog bdw!

I got a chance to be a barista too. I never imagined making coffees could be so much fun. I hope to have my own coffee corner in my own house someday.

Here's my first ever iced caramel macchiato I made.

I'm also in my obsessed-with-boba phase. Do try rose milk and ttaro milk tea with boba and mango jelly - please do - absolute banger.

A great acheievement for someone who used to get everything in my hands, I learned to cook healthy meals.

I'm a firm believer that everything that is meant to happen will happen. You will get what you are destined for. You need to continue putting in efforts and go head fast towards your goal. Everything will fall into place. "Believe in Yourself" works like magic.

Thank you for scrolling till the end! I wish you the best and may you achieve your dreams. Fly High!
